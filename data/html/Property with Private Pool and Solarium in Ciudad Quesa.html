    Ciudad Quesada - Rojales. Alicante Quad house - Sale

    Property with Private Pool and Solarium in Ciudad Quesada

    Property distributed over two floors plus a solarium, located on a private plot with garden and private pool, within a residential complex with two communal swimming pools in Ciudad Quesada (Rojales).

    The ground floor features the private plot with terrace and pool, a glazed entrance porch, living-dining area, separate kitchen with brand-new cabinetry and black marble worktop, and a full bathroom.

    The first floor offers three bedrooms, the main bedroom with built-in wardrobe and access to a glazed terrace, as well as a second full bathroom.

    The upper floor includes a 25 m² outdoor solarium and an 8 m² glazed tower-style space, ideal as a storage room or multipurpose area.

    Located close to supermarkets, natural areas and all amenities, just minutes from Rojales town centre and the beaches.
